How to Read This Dashboard

  • Standings ≠ real-game record. CSL counts forfeit games (marked "NO GAME") as 5-0 wins in standings. Per-game metrics on this dashboard use only matches that were actually played.
  • "Form (last 5 real)" excludes forfeits — it reflects how the team is actually playing.
  • Strength of Schedule is the average PPG of opponents faced. League average is roughly 1.4. Lower = easier opposition played to date.
  • Friday Youth brackets mix boys, girls, and coed teams in the same standings. Rank position is league-wide, not gendered.
  • Snapshot data as of May 7, 2026. Several recent matchdays show games on schedule but no scores posted yet — those are excluded from form calculations until scored.
  • Predictions are commentary, not bets. Calibrated against opponent rank, current form, and SoS.
